Combinators

Descendant (space)

This paragraph is right after the h2 above — it gets the h2 + p styling (bold teal).

The post wraps an emphasized phrase here.

Even nested em inside a blockquote is matched by .post em — the descendant combinator goes any depth.

Child (>)

  • Top item — direct child, gets the yellow background
  • Top item — also direct child
    • Nested item — NOT a direct child, no yellow
    • Nested item — same
  • Last top item

General sibling (~)

FIRST paragraph (the marker).

I follow .first in the same parent — purple.

I follow .first too — also purple.

I'm a div between the paragraphs (still siblings).

Still purple — ~ doesn't care about gaps, only "after, same parent".
